About the Project

The Idea

This application is built around a simple but powerful concept: supporting your wishes through short periods of discipline.

In many cultures, people practice temporary self-restraint or “ascetic commitments” to strengthen their intention toward something meaningful. The idea is that when we consciously give up something or practice discipline for a limited time, we focus our energy and attention on what truly matters to us.

This app brings that idea into a modern, simple digital experience.

Instead of just writing down goals or wishes, you actively support them through small, intentional actions.

How It Works

The process is simple:

  1. Create a Wish
    Start by writing down something meaningful you would like to happen in your life. It could be a new job, improved health, personal growth, or any other intention.
  2. Make a Commitment
    Choose a short discipline challenge that you will follow for a number of days. Examples:
    • No chocolate for 7 days
    • No alcohol for 10 days
    • Exercise every day for 14 days
    • Limit social media use
  3. Check In Daily
    Each day you confirm whether you kept your promise. This builds a streak and keeps you accountable.
  4. Complete the Challenge
    If you successfully complete the commitment period, you have symbolically supported your wish through discipline and focus.
  5. Close the Wish When It Comes True
    The wish can stay open as long as needed. When it eventually happens, you can return to the app and mark it as fulfilled.

Why This Helps

This approach works because it combines several powerful psychological principles:

Focus
A commitment reminds you daily about what truly matters to you.

Discipline
Short challenges help build self-control and consistency.

Accountability
Daily check-ins create awareness of your actions.

Motivation
Watching your progress grow keeps you engaged and inspired.

Reflection
When your wish eventually comes true, you can look back at the effort you invested.

A Personal Journey

This app is not just about tracking habits. It is about creating a meaningful connection between your actions and your intentions.

Each wish becomes a small journey.

Over time, you build a collection of wishes, commitments, and stories of personal growth.

Share Your Progress

You can also share your progress with others using visual cards that show your discipline streak and challenge progress.

This makes it easier to celebrate milestones and stay motivated.

Start Small

You do not need a big goal to begin.

Start with a small wish and a short commitment. Even a few days of discipline can create a powerful sense of progress.
